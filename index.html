About Me

___________________

Hi, my name is Jonathan Zylberberg. Growing up I formed a love for technology by playing multiple video games, like Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles 2, after school on my parents Atari ST. When my family got our first desktop computer I was fascinated by it, and would spend as much time as I could exploring the web and watching funny videos. I saw this interest as a side passion, and when I attended NYU I majored with a B.A. in Economics. I enjoyed Economics but I kept getting pulled to my passion of technology and after graduation I decided to take the plunge in becoming a developer.

I attended The Flatiron School to become a Full Stack Web Developer. I love writing code because I enjoy the ability to create amazing things and nothing makes me happier than seeing people use applications I create. I loved my time at Flatiron learning how to code and now I really want to continue growing my passion and skills for the work I immensly love to do.

Outside of coding I love to play sports, travel (the photo on the main page I took in Japan), and play video games. Currently I am working on side projects and looking for a job. If you would like to speak to me about a potential job opportunity, coding, or anything feel free to contact me at your earliest convenience.
