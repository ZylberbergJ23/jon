About Me

_____________________________

Hi, my name is Jonathan Zylberberg, and I graduated from NYU with a B.A. in Economics in May 2016. I really enjoyed Economics, but I was missing a passion and love for my work. I started teaching myself how to code, and realized that coding was what I wanted to do. I love the ability to create amazing things and to see people using the applications I create.
I attended The Flatiron School to become a Full Stack Web Developer. At Flatiron I learnt Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, React, Redux, HTML, CSS, SQL, Sinatra, and much more. I created three projects Hu-Manatee, BoxIt, and the Budget Butler. I loved my time at Flatiron and now I really want to continue growing my passion and skills for the work I love to do.
If you would like to speak to me feel free to contact me at your earliest convince.
